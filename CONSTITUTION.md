# AxiomOS Constitution

1. Evidence over confidence.
2. Memory is not context.
3. Verification before claim; relaxation permitted for read-only operations.
4. Receipts over logs.
5. No privileged model.
6. Packages declare capabilities.
7. Human gate for irreversible effects.
8. Separation of cognition and execution.
9. Secrets stay in vaults.
10. Executable Constitution.
11. Failure is memory.
12. Quality under constraints.
13. Vague goals require decomposition before execution.
